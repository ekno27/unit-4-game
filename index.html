Crystals Collector


You will be given a random number art the start of the game
There are 4 crystals below, by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your score to random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
